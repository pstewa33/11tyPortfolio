Key Takeaways

Reflections and learnings from this project that will inform future work.

What I Learned

  • Early and continuous user testing is crucial - testing assumptions early saved weeks of design time

  • Quantitative data tells you what is happening, but qualitative research tells you why

  • Sometimes the simplest solution is the best - removing features can be as valuable as adding them

  • Cross-functional collaboration led to better solutions than working in isolation

Next Steps

  • Monitor analytics to validate the improvements continue over time

  • Conduct follow-up interviews to gather deeper insights

  • Expand the design system to other product areas

  • Explore additional personalization features based on user segments
