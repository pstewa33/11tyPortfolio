<section class="technical-approach">
    <h2 id="tech-approach">Technical Approach</h2>
    <hr>
    <p>The prototype was built using the existing internal component library to ensure consistency with production systems. Components were extended with brand-specific styling while preserving their accessibility and structural patterns.</p>
    <p>Content was delivered dynamically by connecting Jekyll templates to Contentful entries, allowing the prototype to simulate a real content workflow rather than relying on static placeholder content.</p>
    <p>The site was deployed to Netlify for easy stakeholder access, enabling the client team to explore the prototype as a working site and provide feedback on navigation, layout, and interactions.</p>
</section>
